    Wan-Peng Zhang 张万鹏¶

    Intro¶

    • Ph.D. student, School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science, Peking University.
    • Email: zawnpn@gmail.com, wpzhang@stu.pku.edu.cn
    • [Google Scholar]

    Biography¶

    • Currently I am a Ph.D. student of School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science, Peking University.

    • I received my M.Sc. degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University in June 2022.

    • I received my B.Sc. degree from School of Mathematical Sciences, Nankai University in June 2019.

    Publications¶

    My research interests include Machine Learning, Reinforcement Learning.
